Adventure Time

Adventure Time is an American animated television series created by Pendleton Ward for Cartoon Network. The series follows the adventures of Finn, a human boy, and his best friend and adoptive brother Jake, a dog with magical powers to change shape and grow and shrink at will. Set in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo, the series explores themes of friendship, adventure, and surreal humor.
